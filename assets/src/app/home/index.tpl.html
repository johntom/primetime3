Meet the Prime Time 3
PrimeTime3

The Prime Time 3 is Orient Point’s longest running Open Party Boat, fishing the North Fork full-time since 1983. The Prime Time 3 is a custom-built 50’ walk-around Fishing Vessel, U.S. Coast Guard certified for 49 passengers and is available for open boat and large group charters, comfortably fishing up to 35 anglers.

Meet the Jenglo
Jenglo

The Jenglo is a 46’ Custom Carolina Sport Fishing Vessel built by master marine architect, Gary Davis. This fishing platform has an excellent layout for groups of 8-24 anglers. With a large cabin and comfortable accommodations, there is generous room for private bottom fishing trips to offshore shark and tuna adventures.

Fishing Trips
Orient By The Sea Marina’s location on the East End of the North Fork offers easy access to the best fishing off of Long Island. Our trips navigate the waters of Long Island Sound, from Plum Gut and the Race to Block Island Sound, Gardiner’s Bay, Peconic Bay and the Atlantic Ocean.
Schedule and Rates
Day Open Boat trips sail at 6AM and 7AM, returning approximately at 2PM and 3PM. Night Open Boat trips sail at 3PM and 4PM, and generally return after sunset. Please see our schedule for current availability of both vessels, and sailing times of Special or Seasonal trips. Private Charter dates are booked through the office, for all information and available dates or should you have any questions regarding Split-Charters or Open Boat trips, please contact our office at 631 323 2618 and speak to Jenny.
Meet the Crew
Creating a highly specialized boutique open boat and sport fishing charter venue, Capt. Mike Boccio and his wife Jenny run the quintessential mom and pop business. Highly successful in organizing professional fishing trips for over 30+ years, they and their crew provide customers with friendly, informative and fun fishing adventures off of beautiful Orient Point.
